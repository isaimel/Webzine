Superman (2025) resurged my love in the superhero. I grew up watching DC content, thus this collection's primary showcase. This is how I have seen superheroes for most of my life, from the Max Fleischer cable broadcasts, to the CW39 morning cartoons, to the award winning graphic novels.

Superheroes are not the same cookie cutter story throughout media, nor do superheroes have to be dark and gritty to tell a provocative story. People are quick to dismiss the genre, either too child-ish or cringe inducing, but it's like ignoring the fantasy, sci-fi, romance, or comedy genre without giving them a try.
